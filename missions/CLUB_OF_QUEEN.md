## What this is

The **Club of Queen** is the process character of the Club suit: **The Arbiter**.
While the Club suit governs *operations, branch structures, the contributor pipeline, and the PR flow*, the Queen rank represents the transformation step of **validation and reconciliation**—auditing code edits, managing review queues, and verifying contribution claims before they are presented to the Spaded Ace (the gatekeeper of the PR boundary).

She ensures operational integrity, resolving conflicts and certifying that pipeline additions are up to standard.
