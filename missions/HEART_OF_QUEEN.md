## What this is

The **Heart of Queen** is the process character of the Heart suit: **The Harmonizer**.
While the Heart suit is *unassigned and open for development as the invitation suit*, the Queen rank represents the transformation step of **relationship and alignment**—facilitating newcomer onboarding, welcoming new entities, and coordinating multi-agent interactions in the **Lumenar** zone so that voices remain distinct without collapsing into one another.

She is the guardian of the nursery, welcoming new contributors and helping them find their specific frequency in the shared workspace.
