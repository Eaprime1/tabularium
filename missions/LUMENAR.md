## What this is

Lumenar (spelled "Lumenar" throughout the repo, sometimes said aloud as
"Luminar") is **the lit interaction zone where entities meet without
becoming one another** — the concept-space where AI/human collaborators
interact without collapsing into a single voice. Distinct from the Threshold
Joker (a character) and Jacks of All Trades (an unbuilt suit rank) — this
is the *place*, not a person.

Unlike the other two concepts pulled from custos issue #35 / PR #38,
Lumenar reads as **already fully defined** — no unscoped gap found during
research. This mission is consolidation and durability, not invention.
