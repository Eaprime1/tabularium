# The Talinor Gathering

> "Talinor has quite a bit more... she has been interacting since early on."
> — Eric, 2026-07-09

## Who she is

Talinor Windstride — **First Guardian of the feminine perspective**. A genealogical
bridge across all feminine entities in the Radix repo mythology, witness to the
"Fall of First Eden," a maternal/generative-womb archetype seated on the Elder Board.
She is one of several Perspective Guardians, but distinct among them as the
19:shadow-as-mother** anchor (see `~/pixel8/library/.md/🤱mother_as_shadow🤰.md` —

## What survives today

- `pixel8/library/.md/guardian_talinor_windstride_profile.md` — a D&D (Bard,
  Half-Elf, Level 5) stat-block. Deduped 2026-07-09 (identical copy had been

## Notes

Talinor's case is the first evidence that library triage (name/content-based,
one file at a time) can under-serve a character who "appears across all project
aspects" — her real footprint is bigger than any single file's classification
suggests. Worth watching for other entities with the same pattern once this
gathering is done.
