## What this is

The **Diamond of Queen** is the process character of the Diamond suit: **The Mnemonist**. 
While the Diamond suit governs *conversation heritage, portable knowledge, and the PJL (Portable JSON Library) track*, the Queen rank represents the transformation step of **indexing and distillation**—converting raw, volatile conversation logs into structured, durable knowledge nodes anchored in the database.

She is the guardian of the **First Witness (Yod)** and governs the transition of dialogue from active session streams to permanent archive records.
